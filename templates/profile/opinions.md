# Author Opinions

Positions and viewpoints you've publicly expressed or consistently hold. Ghostink uses these to maintain consistency across articles — avoiding contradictions with your established stance.

Format:
```
### [Topic]
**Position:** [your stance in one sentence]
**Nuance:** [any caveats or conditions]
**First expressed:** [approximate date or article, if known]
```

---

### [Example: Remote work]
**Position:** [e.g., "Remote work is better for deep work but worse for junior employees who need mentorship"]
**Nuance:** [e.g., "Depends on company culture and role type"]
**First expressed:** [e.g., "2024-03"]
